Have a closer look at the DTA Leadership.

Sanjay Tikku

Founder & CEO

Sanjay founded DTA to offer custom database expertise backed by a level of personal service that he felt was missing in the marketplace. He has helped clients design and build Oracle and SQL Server based systems for high-traffic websites in many different industries including financial services, manufacturing, Internet services, export-import, hospitality, retail and technology services.

Sanjay has worked on Oracle database related projects for over 15 years and has been working in the IT industry for over 25 years. He is an expert at providing time-critical database solutions to corporations around the globe - whether they are experiencing performance and stability related crises with their Oracle databases, or they need out-of-the box design help for a complex Oracle database.

Database Administration Services

Shawn Tozeski is the Chief Managed Services Officer at DTA. Shawn has over 20 years of experience in the IT field working with Oracle databases and supporting mission critical systems for Fortune 500 companies.

His focus is leveraging cloud technologies, and designing and implementing highly available and disaster tolerant systems. He has extensive knowledge in Oracle database performance tuning and backup and recovery.

James Franco

Database Consulting Services

Jim Franco is the Chief Consulting Officer at DTA and an Oracle DBA Certified Master. Jim has worked for over 15 years specializing in areas of Oracle database administration, design and development.

Jim has extensive, hands-on experience with several advanced features of Oracle, including Data Warehousing for Very Large Databases (VLDBs), Oracle GRID, Oracle Advanced Security for HIPAA compliant databases, Oracle Real Application Clusters (RAC), Oracle Data Guard (DG), Enterprise Manager Cloud Control (EMCC) and Oracle XML DB. He is proficient in the realm of the PostgreSQL database for development and administration, and has a strong background in database-related application development.

Neeraj Yadav

Database Development

Neeraj Yadav is the Team Lead – Database Development at DTA and SQL Server Specialist with MCITP. Neeraj has 10 years’ experience, specializing in areas of SQL server development, store procedures, error handling, views, cursor, triggers, and functions, as well as troubleshooting and optimizing SSIS package performance. Neeraj has extensive, hands-on experience with Data Warehousing for Very Large Databases (VLDBs), installing and configuring reporting services, SSMA for data migration, query optimization and performance issues.

Neeraj is proficient in the NoSQL Database as MongoDB for development and has a good understanding of Apache Hadoop and Apache Spark with Scala, Sqoop and Hive. He has a strong background in database related application development.
