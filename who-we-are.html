The DTA Advantage.

For over two decades, we have been redefining the service business of data.

All businesses evolve and with this comes the evolution of how businesses manage, store and work with their data.  DTA was founded on this idea, and has been evolving alongside businesses for over two decades.

This is what we call the DTA Advantage; a philosophy of expansive understanding of how the needs of businesses are constantly shifting, and a mandate to stay on the forefront of service development.  We know that data management goes far beyond just database maintenance. (Although, of course, that is essential too!)  This is why we have developed 24-hour, 7-day a week work processes that we customize to our clients' specific essential needs.

Our teams in the USA and India represent the best comprehensive data experts assembled.  We're excited to bring the DTA Advantage to your business.
