We're there every day of your data life cycle,
24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Data brings life to your enterprise.  Just like real life, it needs to be managed every hour of every day.  This is where DTA's approach is truly unrivaled; we have been experts in 24-hour management throughout every phase of the Information Life Cycle.  With offices in the USA and India, we are able to keep one step ahead of sunrise, allowing us to provide efficiency and insight at an unrivaled level.

Yet, data only comes to life when it engages the human factor of business.  This is why we create an engaged relationship with your enterprise, learning the nuances of your information needs.  Our management strategy is geared towards directing, as much as servicing, the environments, flows and governance of your data in ways that are both efficient and intuitive to your production environment.

We suggest you take a look at how we define the Information Life Cycle, which will help illustrate the scope we bring to data services.

we bring advantages to a field that continues to evolve.

We call it The DTA Advantage, and it has been the core of our service reputation for over two decades.  It is a focus on the broadest range of possibilities combined with a nuanced understanding of integrations between business, technology, and the information which brings them both to life.

In a field too often limited by niche thinking, DTA has made it a mission to always perceive the business of data as a field of constant evolution and growth.  Solutions change with technology; integrations redevelop with growth.  The job is never "done," even though milestones must be reached and objectives completed.  It is this philosophy of approach that has dictated the design of our company, from our 24/7/365 service model to our knowledge-hybridized teamwork approach.

Have a look at the ways the DTA Advantage works for our clients.  We're certain that the savvy and insight we bring will help your company's information integrations result in growth for your enterprise.
